## What are Micro-Breaks?

Micro-breaks are brief 1-minute breaks that fire every **20 minutes** during your coding session. They're designed to prevent digital eye strain, repetitive stress injuries, and postural fatigue from prolonged screen time.

These quick interventions target:
- **Eye health** — reducing strain from constant focus on screens
- **Wrist and hand health** — preventing carpal tunnel and RSI
- **Neck and shoulder tension** — reversing forward head posture

<Note>
The 20-minute interval is based on the **20-20-20 rule** endorsed by the American Academy of Ophthalmology for reducing digital eye strain.
</Note>

## The 20-20-20 Rule

The 20-20-20 rule is a simple technique to prevent eye fatigue:

- Every **20 minutes** of screen time
- Look at something **20 feet away** (or more)
- For at least **20 seconds**

This gives your eye muscles a break from the constant near-focus required for screen work. Following this rule can significantly reduce symptoms like dry eyes, blurred vision, and headaches.

<Tip>
Combine the 20-20-20 rule with rapid blinking (15-20 times) to re-lubricate your eyes after each break.
</Tip>

## Example Micro-Break Tips

Vibe Check rotates through a variety of micro-break exercises. Here are some examples from the plugin:

### 👁️ Eye Reset
> Look at something 20+ feet away for 20 seconds (the 20-20-20 rule). Then blink rapidly 15 times to re-lubricate your eyes.

### 🤲 Wrist Stretch
> Extend one arm, palm up. Gently pull fingers back for 15 sec. Flip palm down, press fingers toward floor for 15 sec. Both sides.

### 🧘 Chin Tuck
> Sit tall. Pull your head straight back (make a double chin). Hold 5 seconds, repeat 5 times. Fixes forward head posture from screen staring.

### 😌 Palming
> Rub your hands together until warm. Cup them gently over your closed eyes for 30 seconds. Breathe deeply. Don't press on your eyeballs.

### 🙆 Shoulder Release
> Raise your shoulders to your ears, hold 3 seconds, then drop them. Roll backward 5 times, forward 5 times. Release that tension.

### 🤸 Prayer Stretch
> Press palms together at chest level. Slowly lower your hands toward your desk keeping palms touching until you feel the stretch. Hold 7 sec, repeat 3 times.

### 🔄 Near-Far Focus
> Hold your thumb 6 inches from your face, focus on it for 5 sec. Shift focus to something 20+ feet away for 5 sec. Repeat 10 times.

## How Reminders Appear

When a micro-break is due, you'll see a reminder card in your Claude conversation:

```
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
⏰ VIBE CHECK — 👁️ Eye Reset (20 min since last break)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Look at something 20+ feet away for 20 seconds (the 20-20-20 rule). Then blink
rapidly 15 times to re-lubricate your eyes.
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
```

Claude will also add a friendly one-sentence encouragement after the reminder box, then seamlessly continue with your coding task.

<Info>
The reminder shows how long it's been since your last break, helping you stay aware of your session duration.
</Info>

## Configuration

You can customize the micro-break interval using environment variables:

```bash
VIBE_CHECK_MICRO_INTERVAL=900  # 15 minutes instead of 20
```

See the [Configuration](/configuration/environment-variables) page for all available options.
