## Overview

Vibe Check includes an on-demand health reference system that you can invoke anytime you need a quick stretch, eye exercise, breathing technique, or ergonomic reminder.

## How to Invoke

Simply type `/vibe-check:health` in your Claude Code session to access the health reference card.

You can also ask about specific topics:
- "show me some stretches"
- "what are good eye exercises?"
- "breathing techniques for stress"
- "how should my desk be set up?"

## What's Included

The health reference card provides instant access to:

| Category | What You Get |
|----------|-------------|
| **Stretches** | 10 quick stretches for neck, wrists, back, chest, shoulders, and hips |
| **Eye Exercises** | 5 exercises to reduce eye strain, including the AAO-endorsed 20-20-20 rule |
| **Breathing Techniques** | 3 science-backed breathing exercises for calm and focus |
| **Ergonomic Checklist** | 7-point setup guide for monitor, chair, posture, and peripherals |

## When to Use It

Use the on-demand health tips when:

- You're feeling stiff or uncomfortable during a long coding session
- Your eyes are feeling strained or dry
- You need a quick stress-relief technique
- You want to check your desk ergonomics
- You've just received an automated reminder and want more detail
- You're setting up a new workspace

<Tip>
Bookmark `/vibe-check:health` for instant access. It works even if you've customized your reminder intervals or disabled automated reminders.
</Tip>
